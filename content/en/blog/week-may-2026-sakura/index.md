# Week of April 27 – May 3, 2026

The main event of this week for me was the cherry blossoms blooming. I live not far from the Biryulyovo Arboretum, and I regularly went to check if they had bloomed. In late April there was severe frost and stormy wind, and I had already resigned myself to not seeing the blossoms, and on Tuesday it even snowed — but I decided to check the grove one more time anyway...

![](image/1.png){width=70%}

It turned out that on that very morning the buds had fully opened, and the snow was falling in large, heavy flakes onto the pink flowers. On a weekday afternoon (!) there were many people in the park. I spent about an hour there, watching passersby admire the sakura and go about their business — only a few photographers remained constant, circling the grove in search of an interesting angle.

![](image/2.png){width=70%}

Overall, the week wasn't very productive in terms of studying, but it was rich in impressions.
